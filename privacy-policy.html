1. Overview

AWS AI Practitioner Prep – AIF-C01 Quiz is an educational tool designed to help users prepare for the AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AIF-C01) examination. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is managed.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Profile — your name and target exam date
  • Mastered questions — used to power Weak Mode tracking
  • Study notes — per-question notes written during sessions
  • Study Reminder settings — your preferred notification time
  • Exam rotation history — tracking questions seen in past simulations
  • Gemini API Key — if provided by you to enable AI features

None of this data is ever transmitted to our servers. Your personal information remains exclusively on your device.

3. AI Features (Google Gemini)

The app offers optional "Deep Dive with AI" features powered by Google Gemini.

  • Use of API Key: Users provide their own Gemini API key. This key is stored securely in your device's local storage and is used only to authenticate requests to Google.
  • Data Transmission: When you request an AI explanation, only the question text and answer options are sent to Google's servers. No personal information or device identifiers are sent with these requests.
  • Data Processing: AI-generated content is provided for educational purposes and should be verified against official AWS documentation.

Once your request reaches Google's servers, it is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and the Gemini API terms.

4. Notifications

The app uses device-local notifications to remind you to study at times you choose. Reminders are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device.

  • No external push servers (e.g., Firebase Cloud Messaging) are used.
  • No device identifier or notification token is collected or transmitted.
  • The RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED permission is used solely to re-register your scheduled study reminders after a device restart, so they continue to fire at the times you chose. It is never used for tracking.

5. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or share any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • No account registration is required
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are sent to our servers
  • No location data is accessed
  • No advertising identifiers are used

7. Third-Party Services

Outside of the optional connection to Google Generative AI (if an API key is provided), this app does not integrate any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or social networking.

8. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because all data is stored locally, there is no risk of data collection from any user.

9. Data Security & Deletion

All data remains exclusively on your local device. You have full control over your data:

  • You can reset specific data (Mastered Questions, Exam History, Spaced Repetition) in the Settings tab.
  • You can delete all application data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device.
