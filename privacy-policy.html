1. Overview

AWS AI Practitioner – AIF-C01 Quiz is an offline practice exam app designed to help users prepare for the AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AIF-C01) examination. This Privacy Policy explains what data, if any, is collected when you use the app.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • No account registration is required
  • No name, email address, or contact details are collected
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are sent to external servers
  • No location data is accessed
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking cookies are used
  • The app does not require an internet connection to function

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Mastered questions — question numbers you have answered correctly, used to power Weak Mode
  • Study notes — per-question notes written during quiz sessions
  • Profile — your name and target exam date (for the countdown banner only)
  • Study Reminder settings — your preferred notification time, days, and enabled state
  • Exam rotation history — which questions have appeared in past Exam Simulation sessions
  • Spaced repetition data — review intervals and history for Smart Study mode
None of this data ever leaves your device. Mastered progress and exam history can be reset using the Reset buttons in the Settings screen. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or social networking. No data is shared with any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children. Because no personal data is collected from any user, there is no additional risk to children.

7. Data Security

Because this app does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no data security risk related to this app. All quiz progress data remains exclusively on your local device.
